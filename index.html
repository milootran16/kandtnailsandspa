Our Story

Welcome to K&T Nails & Spa

Since opening our doors in January 2022, K&T Nails and Spa has become Cypress's premier destination for luxury nail care and relaxation. Conveniently located next to Costco at 5353 Katella Ave., we've built a reputation for exceptional service, impeccable hygiene standards, and creating a welcoming atmosphere where every client feels like family.

Our experienced team of nail technicians combines artistic skill with the latest techniques to deliver stunning results. We pride ourselves on using only premium products and maintaining the highest sanitation standards. Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or simply treating yourself to some well-deserved pampering, we're here to make you look and feel your absolute best.
